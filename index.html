Saved Pet Rescue

Our Mission

We are committed to helping abandoned canines find loving families. Our rescue prioritizes senior animals and those with serious health conditions that are not as likely to be adopted from larger Humane Societies. Dogs with special needs are placed in foster homes where they can receive around the clock attention. Dogs with "behavioral issues" work closely with our trainers and follow a modification plan to help them become a better fit for your family!

These efforts would not be possible without our incredible volunteer network. If you are interested in volunteering please visit the contact page. Also, this is a fictional rescue for demonstration purposes. The donation link connects to a real rescue.
